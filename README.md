**LocalFixer – Local Service Provider Platform**

LocalFixer is a web-based platform designed to connect users with trusted local service providers such as plumbers, electricians, carpenters, cleaners, 
and technicians. The platform allows customers to easily search for nearby professionals, view their details, and book services according to their needs. 
It aims to simplify the process of finding reliable local services while helping service providers reach more customers. LocalFixer offers a user-friendly
interface, efficient service management, and a convenient way to bridge the gap between customers and local professionals.
